My Recommendation: A (clean list menu)

Rationale: A menu list feels human and approachable — like a restaurant, not a SaaS checkout. The copy actually sells what each tier delivers instead of just labeling them. "I come to you" and "Walk away with 3-5 workflows you can use Monday morning" are concrete. The free offer naturally leads into the paid tiers without a hard sell. And "Larger programs available — let's talk" catches the enterprise fish without cluttering the menu.
