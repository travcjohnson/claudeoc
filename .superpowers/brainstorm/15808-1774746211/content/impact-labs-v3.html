Problems that shouldn't still exist
These are the kinds of things we want to work on. Sound familiar?
🚨
Human trafficking reports stuck in silos
Tips go to different agencies with no shared system. Patterns get missed. What if one tool connected the dots across jurisdictions?
🏠
Homeless shelter beds going unfilled
Shelters have open beds. Outreach workers can't see them. A real-time availability tool could match people to beds in minutes, not days.
👶
Foster care paperwork that delays placement
A child waits weeks while forms move between offices. An AI assistant could pre-fill, route, and track every document — getting kids placed faster.
💊
Mental health resources nobody can find
211 lines are overwhelmed. Directories are outdated. What if someone in crisis could describe what they need and get matched to the right resource instantly?
📋
Nonprofit grant reporting that eats staff time
Small nonprofits spend 30% of their time on compliance paperwork instead of serving people. An AI tool could draft reports from their existing data in minutes.
