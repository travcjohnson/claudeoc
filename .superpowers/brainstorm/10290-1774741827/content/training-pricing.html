C

Free hero + "Inquire" only (no public pricing)

Free offer dominates. Paid tiers described but no dollar amounts on the page. Forces a conversation, lets you price based on the org.

Pro: Maximum flexibility on pricing per client
Con: Creates friction — CEOs want to know the ballpark before reaching out
My Recommendation: A (full tier card)

Rationale: Brandon's framework is clean and escalates naturally. Showing "from" pricing builds credibility — you're a real business, not guessing. The $5K/hr corporate 1:1 rate is a power signal (it says "my time is worth it"). The free offer still dominates as the entry point. CEOs who see the paid tiers understand you're not just giving stuff away — the free session IS the pitch. And the retainer "inquire" keeps your biggest deals flexible.

One thought: the $5K/hr corporate 1:1 is bold (and justified for C-suite access). But showing it publicly might scare off mid-market. Consider showing it as "$5,000 minimum" rather than "/hr" — same economics, different framing. The exec doesn't need to know it's an hour rate.
