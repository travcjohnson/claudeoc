What we do

One studio, every phase of your landscape.

No juggling designers, contractors, and subs. Our in-house team carries your project from concept drawings through the final plant in the ground.

Landscape Design

Concept plans, 3D renderings, and planting palettes tailored to your architecture, sun exposure, and how your family uses the yard.

Hardscape & Outdoor Living

Patios, seat walls, fire features, pergolas, and outdoor kitchens — engineered and built by our own crews, not subbed out.

Drought-Smart Planting

California-native and Mediterranean gardens with drip irrigation and smart controllers — beautiful in July, gentle on the water bill.

Lighting & Irrigation

Low-voltage landscape lighting and water-wise irrigation retrofits that extend your evenings outside and cut water use up to 40%.
